## 1. WHAT IT IS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

X-Ray Forensic (xrayforensic.com) is a forensic trade-behavior diagnostic SaaS.

It analyzes a trader's MT5 trade history and tells them **WHY they lose money** — not just that they lost. It diagnoses behavioral patterns, session discipline, risk architecture, and the institutional concepts the trader is violating, then gives specific, measurable prescriptions to fix them.

**The one-line pitch:**
"The only platform that tells you WHY you lose — not just that you lost."

**The mission:**
Turn losing traders into consistently profitable ones — by showing them exactly what's destroying their edge and holding them accountable to fixing it. Not tips. Not signals. Diagnosis, prescription, accountability, results.

## 3. THE PRODUCT — HOW IT WORKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Trader exports their MT5 trade history (.csv, .htm, .html, .xlsx, or .xml)
2. Uploads it to X-Ray
3. The forensic engine scores them on 7 behavioral dimensions
4. Generates a report with archetype, leaks (in dollars), and ranked prescriptions
5. Trader fixes one thing, re-uploads, sees the improvement arc

**The 7 Diagnostic Dimensions (scored 0-100):**
1. HTF Bias Alignment — trading with or against higher-timeframe structure
2. Liquidity Awareness — stop placement relative to liquidity pools
3. OTE Zone Discipline — entry quality
4. OB/FVG Confluence — institutional entry confluence
5. Session Discipline — trading in the right kill zones (London/NY)
6. Risk Architecture — sizing, stop-loss usage, risk-reward
7. Behavioral Control — revenge trading, overconfidence, streak bias

**Trader Archetypes detected:**
disciplined_sniper, revenge_chaser, session_gambler/tourist, overtrader, risk_mismanager, improving_student, institutional
